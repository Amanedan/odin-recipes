Spaghetti

Spaghetti is one of the staple dishes most north american families ate quite a bit growing up, I myself was a difficult child and would only want to eat pasta, so to say I ate alot of it.

My family would make it the cheap way, no fancy sauce with a bunch of stuff like gross veggies mixed in, just a simple 4 ingredient pasta that tasted amazing. On this page I will tell you how we made it, and you can give it a try yourself and let me know by getting in touch at the bottom of the page.
