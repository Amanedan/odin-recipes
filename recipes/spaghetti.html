Spaghetti

Spaghetti is one of the staple dishes most north american families ate quite a bit growing up, I myself was a difficult child and would only want to eat pasta, so to say I ate alot of it.

My family would make it the cheap way, no fancy sauce with a bunch of stuff like gross veggies mixed in, just a simple 4 ingredient pasta that tasted amazing. On this page I will tell you how we made it, and you can give it a try yourself and let me know by getting in touch at the bottom of the page.

Ingredients

Lets start with getting the ingredients that you're going to need

  • Cambells tomato soup
  • Spaghetti nooddles
  • Ground beef
  • Salt

With these 4 ingredients you will make your own little bowl of heaven, Dont forget your cookware!

You will need:

  • 1 Strainer
  • 1 Cooking pot
  • 1 Frying pan
  • 2 Utensils of your choice to mix and cook with

Steps

  1. First start boiling your water and add salt until it tastes like the ocean
  2. Secondly take your beef and start browing it in your frying pan, make sure its cooked through and theres no pink!
  3. Next your going to get your sauce ready by opening up the can of tomato soup
  4. When the water is salty and boiling your going to add your pasta and cook until its to your liking, I like mine to have a bit of a bite to it but if your like yours to have no texutre thats your taste
  5. Once your beef is browned drain the greast (not into the sink) and add the soup straight into the pan and start mixing
  6. Once the pasta is ready strain it out and dump the sauce, (the ground beef and soup mix) into the pot and mix it all together

That's it, that's all. Now you have great spaghetti!
